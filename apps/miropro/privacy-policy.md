# Privacy Policy for MiroPro

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the MiroPro mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how data is processed.

---

## Information We Access

The App may request access to the following:

- **Camera** – Used for real-time mirror display, selfie capture, and magnifier functionality.
- **Local Storage** – Used to store basic application data and settings.
- **Photo Library** – Accessed only when you choose to save a photo taken within the App.

If you enable iCloud synchronization, certain data may be securely stored in your personal iCloud account. We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data to our own servers.

---

## How Information Is Used

- All data processing is performed locally on your device.
- We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information to external servers.
- Camera access is used solely for real-time display and photo capture.
- Photos are saved to your device’s photo library only if you explicitly choose to save them.

---

## Data Storage and Security

- Most data is stored locally on your device.
- If iCloud synchronization is enabled, synchronized data is encrypted and stored within your personal iCloud account, protected under Apple’s privacy policies.
- The App does not share data with third parties.
- No data is collected for analytics or advertising purposes.
- Camera data is used only for real-time display and is not stored unless you explicitly save an image.
- The App securely stores data locally on your device.

---

## iCloud Synchronization

- Synchronization occurs only when you are signed into your iCloud account.
- Synchronized data is protected under Apple’s privacy policy.
- You may disable iCloud synchronization at any time in your device settings.
- Notes and related data are encrypted and stored in your iCloud account via CloudKit.
- If synchronization is disabled, your data remains stored locally on your device.

---

## Photo Library Access

- The App requests photo library access only when you choose to save a photo.
- Access is used solely to save images generated within the App.
- We do not scan, upload, or analyze the contents of your photo library.
- When using the mirror selfie feature, the App requests permission only to save captured photos.
- All saved photos remain on your device and are not uploaded to any external servers.

---

## Your Rights

You may:

- Manage app permissions through your device settings.
- Delete all App data by uninstalling the App.

---

## Privacy for Applicable Audiences

The App is suitable for users aged 4 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from any users.

---

## Privacy Policy Update Notifications

If this Privacy Policy is updated, we will notify users within the App or through other appropriate means.

---

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at [ chat@imp.chat ].


# 隐私政策 — 补光镜MiroPro

最后更新日期：2026-02-14

## 简介

本隐私政策说明您在使用本应用时，我们如何访问、使用及保护相关信息。

我们致力于保护您的隐私，并确保数据处理方式透明、安全。

---

## 我们访问的信息

本应用可能请求以下权限：

- **相机权限** —— 用于实时镜像显示、自拍及放大镜功能  
- **本地存储权限** —— 用于保存基本应用功能数据和设置  
- **照片库权限** —— 仅在您选择保存照片时用于存储拍摄图片  

如您启用 iCloud，同步数据将加密后存储在您的 iCloud 账户中。

我们不会收集、存储或传输任何用户数据至我们自己的服务器。

---

## 信息的使用方式

- 所有数据处理均在您的设备本地完成  
- 我们不会收集、存储或传输任何个人信息至外部服务器  
- 相机访问仅用于实时显示和拍摄照片  
- 仅当您主动选择保存时，照片才会存储至您的设备相册  

---

## 数据存储与安全

- 大部分数据仅存储在您的设备本地  
- 如您启用 iCloud，同步数据将加密后存储在您的 iCloud 账户中，并受 Apple 隐私政策保护  
- 不与任何第三方共享数据  
- 不收集用于分析或广告的数据  
- 相机数据仅用于实时显示，除非您主动选择保存，否则不会存储任何图像数据  
- 应用将数据安全地存储在本地设备

---

## iCloud 同步

- 仅在您登录 iCloud 账户时进行数据同步  
- 同步数据受 Apple 隐私政策保护  
- 您可随时在设备设置中禁用 iCloud 同步  
- 笔记数据通过 CloudKit 加密存储在您的 iCloud 账户中  
- 禁用同步后，相关数据将仅保存在本地设备  

---

## 照片库访问

- 仅在您选择保存照片时请求照片库访问权限  
- 访问权限仅用于保存应用内生成的图片  
- 我们不会扫描、上传或分析您的照片库内容  
- 当您使用镜像自拍功能时，应用会请求照片库访问权限以保存拍摄的照片  
- 所有照片均保存在您的设备本地，不会上传至任何服务器  

---

## 您的权利

您可以：

- 通过设备设置管理应用权限  
- 卸载应用以删除所有本地应用数据  

---

## 适用人群隐私

本应用适用于 4 岁及以上所有用户。  
我们不会主动收集任何用户的个人信息。

---

## 隐私政策更新

如隐私政策有更新，我们将在应用内或通过其他适当方式通知您。

---

## 联系我们

如有任何关于本隐私政策的问题，请通过 [ chat@imp.chat ] 联系我们。
