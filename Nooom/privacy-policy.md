Privacy Policy for Nooom

Last Updated: May 8, 2026

Nooom ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information Collection and Use Nooom is designed with a privacy-focused approach. On-Device Processing · Core features of the App operate on your device · No account or sign-in is required

Images and Content When you use image analysis features: · Images you capture or upload may be sent to third-party services (such as Google Gemini API) for processing · We do not store your images on our own servers · Image data is processed only for the purpose of providing app functionality

Usage Data We may collect limited, non-identifiable usage data to improve the App, such as: · Feature usage · Basic interaction events This data is used in an aggregated and anonymized manner.

Advertising and Tracking Third-Party Advertising The App uses advertising services such as Google AdMob to display ads. These services may collect information including: · Device identifiers (such as IDFA) · Approximate location · App interaction data This information is used to: · Deliver and personalize advertisements · Measure ad performance

App Tracking Transparency (ATT) On iOS 14.5 and later: We will request your permission before enabling tracking across apps and websites You can allow or deny tracking through the system prompt You can change your preference at any time in your device settings

Third-Party Services The App relies on the following third-party services: Google Gemini API — for image recognition and language processing Google AdMob — for advertising These services operate under their own privacy policies. We do not control how they collect or use data.

Data Storage and Security We do not store personal data on our own servers Data processed by third-party services is subject to their security practices We implement reasonable measures to protect information However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Your Rights and Choices You can: Control tracking permissions through iOS settings Manage advertising preferences via your device Revoke permissions (such as camera access) at any time

Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the App or on our website The updated date will be revised accordingly Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
